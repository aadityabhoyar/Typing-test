About

This typing test is designed to be minimalistic and customizable, featuring various test modes to suit different needs. It provides real-time feedback on typos, speed, and accuracy, emulating the experience of natural keyboard typing. Test yourself in different modes, track your progress, and improve your typing speed.


Word Set

We use the Quotes API to generate the tests, ensuring a diverse and challenging set of words for each test.


Stats

WPM: Words Per Minute - a measure of your typing speed.
Accuracy: Typing accuracy percentage, indicating the proportion of correct keystrokes.
Mistakes: The number of typos made during the test.


Results Screen

After completing a test, you will see detailed results including your WPM, accuracy, and mistakes. Additionally, a graph of your WPM and accuracy over time is available to help you track your progress and identify areas for improvement.
